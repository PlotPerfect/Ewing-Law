Ewing Law: Leading Criminal Defence Solicitors in London

Established in 2013 by Principal Solicitor Scott Ewing, Ewing Law has rapidly become one of the most respected criminal defence firms in the UK. Scott Ewing, a nationally renowned criminal solicitor with nearly thirty years of experience, leads a dedicated team of highly skilled and like-minded lawyers. Our firm’s reputation for excellence, integrity, and discretion is recognised throughout the legal profession and by clients nationwide.

At Ewing Law, we specialise in providing expert legal representation for a wide range of criminal matters. Our team has successfully defended clients facing the most serious prosecutions, including multi-handed murders, complex fraud cases, white collar crime, regulatory offences, and private motoring offences. We are adept at handling cases involving multi-jurisdictional issues and have extensive experience working with both individuals and corporate clients.

Every case we take on, regardless of its size or complexity, receives our full attention and commitment. We pride ourselves on meticulous case preparation, honest and realistic legal advice, and a client-focused approach that prioritises your best interests at every stage. Our long-standing relationships with leading Chambers in London, Birmingham, and Liverpool give us access to top-ranked barristers, ensuring that our clients benefit from the highest standard of courtroom advocacy.

Ewing Law is widely regarded as a leading choice for anyone seeking expert criminal defence solicitors in London and across the UK. Our proven track record, combined with our unwavering commitment to client care, sets us apart from other law firms. We understand that facing a criminal investigation can be daunting and stressful. That’s why we offer clear communication, absolute discretion, and robust defence strategies tailored to your unique circumstances.

If you or someone you know is under investigation or facing prosecution, do not hesitate to contact Ewing Law. Our experienced team is ready to provide immediate advice and support. Reach out to us at enquiries@ewinglaw.co.uk or call us on 0207 183 8838 for a confidential consultation. Trust Ewing Law to protect your rights and deliver the best possible outcome for your case.
